# Text Elements
1. Enable developer mode in the Meta Quest mobile app on your phone or tablet. To get there, tap Menu->Devices->Developer Mode and toggle it to on. ^Pjl2JxxS

2. Download and install SideQuest, if you don’t already have it. This program allows you to sideload apps onto the headset. ^CUEce8Hu

3. Launch SideQuest with the Quest 2 plugged into your PC via USB. The app should detect your headset automatically. If this is the first time, you will see a prompt on the headset to allow USB debugging ^DdncWntu

4. Enter “questcraft” in the SideQuest search box and click the top result. ^9gltQrpN

5. Click Download App (Sideload). It will download and install. A status bar will quickly show when it has finished but then disappear. In our case, the process took less than 10 seconds. ^7D3GvRFe

6. Disconnect the headset and put it on. ^Mjw0FwTp

7. Navigate to the apps menu on the Quest 2. ^fbR374ez

8. Click the filter button in the upper right corner. ^wDqTdf1s

9. Select “Unknown sources” from the pulldown menu. ^QtS4s5kU

10. Select QuestCraft to load it. ^khiDj1zC

11. Click Login on the QuestCraft splash screen and follow the instructions to sign in. ^AEhClWaq

12. Click Play. ^Cz0YZfP4

It will take a few moments to download assets and load the game. The screen will turn black for a bit while it's loading. Don’t take off the headset or quit. When it shows you in a box made of Minecraft dirt blocks, it’s almost done loading. ^HsSLmFaK
